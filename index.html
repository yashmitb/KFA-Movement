Background

The lack of education in children these days is a major problem that can lead to the students falling deep into poverty, crime, or worse. This campaign is aimed toward those 1 in 5 students who don't get a chance for education. These students lose a lot out of life from not getting a good education. One such effect is a lack of voice. Due to a lack of self-confidence from not going to school and absence of information and basic knowledge, many uneducated people are oppressed and thus taken advantage of. An example of this is in women for developing countries, who are often oppressed due to being dependent on the male figures surrounding them. Another side effect of lack of education is falling into a poverty trap. Children of poor parents end up not being able to go to school and most probably end up in poverty too. A Brookings Institute study found that one year of education leads to a 10% growth in earnings. Not only a lower wage range, but also unemployment is a big after effect of this issue. The more education you have, the more likely you are to be hired. These are just some of the aftereffects people with a lack of education face. Our goal is a reasonable one and one that should already have been implemented worldwide. This can be seen in article 26 of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights which states, “Everyone has the right to education. Education shall be free, at least in the elementary and fundamental stages. Elementary education shall be compulsory.” Thus this declaration supports our goal of providing education to everyone. Our goal is for everyone to have free education for all the primary schools and allow students to pursue their dream jobs. We are going to do this by first seeing the outcome of smaller cities and towns, seeing if they are effective or not, and they keep going until everyone in the primary schools gets the education they need. We will then aim for primary school education at a national and international level. We plan to also protest decreasing the cost of colleges and universities for students.

Call To Action

The KFA organization's goal is to get more children and teenagers under 18 a fair education and the right to a better life. To accomplish this goal, we will work on getting laws passed that reduce the cost of education, raising money to put kids in poorer communities in school, and raise awareness for this issue.

Objective

Our main objective and end goal is to have a thriving world where children can go to school and learn to improve their and their family’s lives. Raising awareness and money is crucial for this dream to come true as without resources and numbers, we cannot hope to take on this huge problem. Raising money will help us to fund individual families in poorer communities to send their kids to school. Having volunteers for our campaign will help in not only raising awareness, but also getting our organization the physical help it needs in carrying out our goals. Politicians and celebrities are important for our organization as to get laws passed to reduce the cost of education, we need people with influence supporting us. Community members can also help by lending a hand to local families who are unable to send their child or children to school.
